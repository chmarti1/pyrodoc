Features of PYroMat

PYroMat provides thermodynamic properties for almost 400 substances. Fast and intuitive access to the properties of each substance comes from calling up objects. Properties are calculated by object methods that support array-like objects as arguments.

>>> import pyromat as pm
>>> air = pm.get('ig.air')
>>> air.gam(T=[300, 400, 500, 600])
array([1.39990859, 1.39519249, 1.38666408, 1.37575277])

The properties include

  • cp Isobaric specific heat
  • cv Isochoric specific heat
  • d Density
  • e Internal energy
  • gam Specific heat ratio
  • h Enthalpy
  • mw Molecular weight
  • R Ideal gas constant
  • s Entropy

As of version 2.0.7, PYroMat includes data for multi-phase carbon dioxide, multi-phase water, and multi-phase refrigerant 134a (C2H2F4 a.k.a. 1,1,1,2- tetrafloroethane). Add in the MANY ideal gas species, and there are 934 species in total.
